Scholar Selection

Scholars are selected through collaboration between instructors and Yoff families, with preference given to students whose families have hosted Bridge Year students in the past, or have been otherwise involved with the Bridge Year program. Students represent a wide range of ages and interests. See our scholar's bio's below to learn more!

Babacar Mbaye

Year: 2nd year of Middle School, 14

Babacar lives in Yoff-Apecsy. He loves music and playing soccer, and he helps a lot around the house.

"I would love to participate in this program to learn to play instruments like the djembe."

Mame Fatou Mbaye

Year: 2nd year of Middle School, 14

"I hope to participate in this program because I love traveling to discover the various cultures within my country and to learn new things."

Fallou Mbow

Year: 2nd year of High School, 17

Fallou lives in central Yoff.

"The excursions are the most interesting [part of the program], such as going to Kolda, Kedougou, or Saint Louis. I would love to learn more about the daily lives of these communities and about issues around education, habitat, transportation, and agriculture."

Elimane Saliou Dia
