Impact of NBYP Cancellation, Covid-19

Princeton’s Novogratz Bridge Year Program in partnership with Where There Be Dragons has sent students to live, learn, and volunteer in Dakar, Senegal for the past 9 years. Unfortunately, the program was cancelled for the ‘20-21 academic year due to the COVID-19 global pandemic. This decision disproportionately affects the financial situation of the program instructors, on site staff, and families who have supported the student participants over the years.

Outside of the communities in Senegal connected to the program, the economic toll on the country has been drastic. The tourism industry and much of the informal economy (transportation, food and grocery stands, home businesses) make up a great part of Senegal's economy and have suffered as a result of Covid-19 restrictions. As of July 28, the total number of confirmed cases rests just below 10,000. Read more about Senegal's coronavirus response here.
